FAQ

Common Questions

🎓
How do I join?
Contact your science teacher or email us directly. Tryouts are held at the start of the fall semester. No prior experience needed — just passion for science!
📅
When do practices occur?
Practices are held weekly after school, typically Tuesday and Thursday. Build event teams may meet additionally on weekends before competitions.
💰
Is there a cost to join?
There is a small participation fee to cover tournament registration and materials. Scholarships available — no student turned away for financial reasons.
🏅
What events should I do?
Events are assigned based on interests and strengths during tryouts. Members typically compete in 2–3 events. Check the Events page for descriptions.
